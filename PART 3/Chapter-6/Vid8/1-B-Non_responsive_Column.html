The scren is taken to be of 12 units. So, col-6 means 6 units, i.e. half width. Similarly, col-3 is 1/4th of scren width, col-2 is 1/6th of scren width, col-1 is 1/12th of scren width.
Note that these "col-n" classed divs are not responsive: they do not re arrange themselves when size is reduced.
